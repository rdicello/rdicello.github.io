About Me

Hi, I'm R.J. DiCello. I'm a 20 year old music producer and computer programmer from Ohio. Right now, I'm a student at Berklee College of Music. I'm very grateful to be here, even though I almost wanted to leave a few months ago.

For the last 2 years, I was torn between music and computer science. I knew I couldn't choose both, so I chose music, and buried computer science. I came to Berklee not sure of myself, so there were many times I felt miserable. I worked to create meaningful projects, even when I hated what I was doing. After 1.5 years, I couldn't handle the confusion, so I took a gap semester. I became a dairy associate, stacking dairy and frozen items on grocery store shelves. I remember looking at myself in the freezer's glass reflection, thinking about what I could be doing at school. It humbled me, making Berklee seem like vacation, and teaching me that I might as well work toward something that I resonate with, even in the slightest. My mind was clearer, realizing that I could go back to Berklee and try again with a different mindset. I would be grateful to learn, be positive, expressive, authentic, courageous, and original.

Now, I'm finishing my committments at Berklee, majoring in Electronic Production and Design and minoring in Creative Coding, and learning about software development and computer science in my spare time. I have big plans for future projects, and I know that I want to carry my morals I listed above into everything I do. Feel free to check out my music below.
